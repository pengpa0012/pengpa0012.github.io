About

Brief Background

I'm a student from Cainta, Rizal Philippines, im currently on my third year of college learning front-end technologies and building static websites. I also design and make layouts for websites from scratch.

My Interest

I really like making stuff by typing code, having to learn something new and applying those techniques on your craft, really expand my creativity and wanting to do some more.

How i started coding...

It started back on 2018 when i saw a student on net cafe where i always play online games, typing some code on sublime editor and building website. I was fascinated back then that by coding you can create your own stuff and let your creativity explore.

I first started to study c++ i learned some of the basic stuff and did some simple programs with it, and then i learned how to build websites using Html and Css i really got into designing stuff because i like styling things and playing around with colors.
